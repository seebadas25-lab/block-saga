How to Play

1. Drag & Drop

Drag blocks from the bottom tray to the grid.

2. Clear Lines

Fill entire rows or columns to clear them and score points.

3. Combo Bonus

Clear lines consecutively to trigger COMBO multipliers!

4. No Rotation

Blocks cannot be rotated. Plan carefully!

Game Over

If you run out of space for new blocks, the game ends.
